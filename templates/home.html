About Us

Welcome to AevolveAI

AI is demystifying the way we look at our world now. We at AevolveAI provides one of the AI application - high accuracy optical character recognition system, that will help the daily business users or any individuals to get on with their life without worrying about time.

Starting as a small business in India 2021, we have big dreams. Our passion for AI, means that we provide our customers with nothing but the highest quality of products, that are guranteed to meet their needs and keep them satisfied.

With motivated team, we strive to be the creative minds that bring smile to you face, That's why we're always looking for innovative new ways to get the best to you.

AevolveAI, established by a software professional, focussing on providing AI solutions to solve daily business problems. AevolveAI provides businesses with services that will help automate their boring and laborious tasks and save lots of computational time. We provide unique services to each client in terms of AI need that will match their requirements. Our AI model is continuously evolving and will offer greater support in all business sectors going forward.

Optical Character recognition

Get the best accuracy editable text document from image without any manual typing(Current Language Support: English, other languages coming soon...).

Able to detect large paragraphs and camera clicks

This OCR system works for both big sentence, paragraphs and random camera clicks. Supports multiple image formats(JPG, JPEG, PNG, etc.).

Text to Microsoft Word document conversion

You can download the text document into a word document as many as you want.

Journey of this startup company

Being a professional, I faced a lot of difficulties processing documents having image text in it. The manual typing takes lot of our valuable time. This made me realize that around the world almost everyone faces this issue and the solutions provided are limited.

This lead me to a deeper investigations and came up with a solutions Artificial Intelligence has - Optical Character Recognition System. And since I am an AI practitioner, unraveling on this matter grown from time to time finding the best algorithms.

What holds the future in Aevolve AI

Exploring the speech recognition system; will be able to translate speech from one language to another. Another one could be speech to text and vice-versa.

Definitely will explore AI based security systems. Have lots of idea about facial recognition systems, and deep diving in GAN.

Here in Aevolve AI we evolve as time goes on.
